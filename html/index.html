Meet Dr. Jugenburg, M.D. FRCSC

Internationally trained Toronto Plastic and Reconstructive Surgeon best known for his:
  • Best Results and Quality Patient Care as noted in our reviews and Before/After
  • Efficient and Experienced Surgical Technique
  • Innovative Approach to Cosmetic Surgery
  • To provide patients with best results and quicker recovery times, Dr. Jugenburg ensures that he is up to date with the most recent and best surgical techniques.

Nurse Kim

Our highly personable Managing Director, Kim McDonald is here to ensure all your needs are taken care of, and all your questions are answered. She arranges and books all procedures and ensures everything is in perfect order for your procedure. kim@torontosurgery.ca

Kim’s 20-year career in health care began when she became a Registered Nurse, graduating from Nursing School as Valedictorian. Kim furthered her career holding progressive positions at a variety of cosmetic and pharmaceutical companies, such as Allergan, the manufacturer of Botox, Juvederm, and Breast Implants.
